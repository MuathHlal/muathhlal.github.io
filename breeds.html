Persian

Persian kittens are known for their long, luxurious coats and sweet personalities. They are calm, gentle, and enjoy a relaxed lifestyle. Persians require regular grooming to maintain their beautiful coats.

Siamese

Siamese kittens are vocal, intelligent, and social. They have striking blue eyes and a distinctive pointed coat pattern. Siamese cats form strong bonds with their owners and love to be involved in all activities.

Maine Coon

Maine Coon kittens are large, friendly giants. They have tufted ears, bushy tails, and water-resistant coats. Known as "gentle giants," they are playful, intelligent, and get along well with children and other pets.

Scottish Fold

Scottish Fold kittens are recognizable by their folded ears and round faces. They have sweet, gentle personalities and adapt well to various living situations. They enjoy human companionship and are relatively quiet cats.

Bengal

Bengal kittens are energetic and athletic with stunning spotted coats that resemble their wild ancestors. They are highly intelligent, curious, and need plenty of stimulation and playtime to stay happy.
